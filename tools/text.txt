Jetan in Space - a brand-new ZX Spectrum Next game, crafted in Assembly with over 200 music tracks.

The game runs in full 320×256 resolution. The blinking red border shows how much space a frame would have taken.

Beat a level once, hop back to it anytime. There are 10 levels in total, so buckle up -  the galaxy has a long to-do list!

There are 3 difficulty levels.


To reach another planet and maybe even get home collect 3 rocket pieces and enough fuel.


At night, visibility is limited

Try to survive as long as you can in the meteor shower! Every second earns you 250 points, and every 65K points grants an extra life. Trust us — you'll want those later. Space does not get friendlier!

Heads up! A sneaky fuel thief sprints for your rocket the moment you refuel.
