 DURING THE GAME, YOU COLLECT THREE
 ROCKET PARTS, AND AFTERWARDS, FUEL.

 ON SOME LEVELS, THERE IS A FUEL THIEF.
 KILL HIM BEFORE HE REACHES THE ROCKET!

 THE JETPACK WILL HEAT UP FROM USAGE.
 YOU CAN SEE ITS TEMPERATURE IN 
 THE UPPER RIGHT CORNER. 
 ONCE IT OVERHEATS, THE JETMAN WILL FLY
 SLOWER. YOU HAVE TO LAND TO COOL IT
 DOWN.

 THE AMOUNT OF FUEL IS SHOWN ON 
 THE FUEL GAUGE. ONCE IT'S FULL, 
 THE ROCKET STARTS BLINKING, AND YOU
 CAN FLY TO THE NEXT LEVEL THROUGH
 THE METEOR SHOWER.

 EACH SECOND SURVIVED IN A METEOR
 SHOWER GIVES 255 POINTS. 
 AN EXTRA LIFE IS GRANTED EVERY 
 60K POINTS.
 PICKING UP ELEMENTS BEFORE THEY HIT 
 THE GROUND GIVES EXTRA POINTS.

 JETMAN CAN BE INVINCIBLE FOR A 
 SHORT PERIOD. IT HAPPENS RIGHT AFTER 
 RESPAWN, OR AFTER PICKING UP THE ARMOR.
 HE WILL BLINK RAPIDLY DURING THIS TIME,
 AND IT WILL SLOW DOWN JUST BEFORE 
 INVINCIBILITY ENDS.
